Why Fractional?

For startups and growing businesses, bringing on a full-time CTO too early can drain precious runway through high salaries and significant equity grants. A fractional CTO and engineering services model provides the best of both worlds—senior-level technical leadership, strategic guidance, and scalable engineering execution—without the long-term financial burden. This approach ensures your technology decisions align with business goals, helps you move faster with the right technical foundation, and preserves more of your equity and capital for what matters most -- getting your product to market.
